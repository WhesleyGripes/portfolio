Our Story

Fire. Flavor. Community.

Ember is built around an open wood-fired kitchen where every dish carries the warmth of real flame. We source locally, cook seasonally, and believe that the best meals happen when people slow down.

Our cocktail bar features handcrafted drinks that complement the bold, smoky flavors of our kitchen -- from classic old fashioneds to seasonal originals.
