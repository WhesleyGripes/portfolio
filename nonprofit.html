Why We Exist

Rise was founded in 2012 with a simple belief: no one should be held back by circumstances beyond their control. What began as a small food drive in a church basement has grown into a movement that spans 28 communities.

"Rise didn't just give me a meal. They gave me a mentor, a job, and the confidence to believe I could build something better." -- Marcus, program graduate

Today, we provide food security, job training, youth mentorship, and housing assistance. 92 cents of every dollar goes directly to programs.

Our Programs

We believe lasting change requires more than a single intervention. Our four pillars work together to address the root causes of poverty and create pathways to self-sufficiency.

Food Security: We operate 12 community pantries and serve over 5,000 meals each week through partnerships with local restaurants and farms.

Job Training: Our workforce development program has placed 850 graduates into full-time employment with an 89% retention rate after one year.

Youth Mentorship: Every young person in our program is paired with a trained mentor. Last year, 96% of our mentees graduated high school on time.

Housing Assistance: We provide emergency housing support and help families navigate the path to stable, long-term housing.
